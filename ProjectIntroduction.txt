My chosen project : Custom cakes site
Customers will be able to customise the form, size, colour, flavour, 
design, and other aspects of their one-of-a-kind cake using a 
user-friendly interactive interface.
Users will be able to build their cake at home and then pick from a
number of cake stores and bakeries to deliver it to.
Major goal of thw site is for users to be able to create a cake that 
is uniquely envisioned and adorned for that once-in-a-lifetime event.
Project Functions:
Customizable flavors
Plethora of new shapes and designs
An auto-make button, to whip a random beautiful cake
A Number of decorative items to embellish your cake
Easy interface
Special options for the elderly, people with allergies (sugar, lactose, peanuts, etc.)
